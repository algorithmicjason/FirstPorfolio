Welcome to, My Brain

The brain that never forgets

My Brain was created to help people securely hoard valuable information into their virtual brain. My Brain can aid the human brain when the human brain cannot remember. Store ideas, thoughts, or memories in My Brain for internal & unlimited memory. Depend on My Brain to bring recollection when you need it most!

My Brain allows you to create multiple folders within your brain to categorize your thoughts. After titling and color-coding your folder, you can then create new wrinkles within your folder to track. Also while creating the wrinkle you can decide if you'd like to share the wrinkle with others using the app, or keep your thought to yourself. If you choose your wrinkle to be public, your post will be added to the Worldly Wrinkles section which can be viewed by every user. Of course if you'd like to keep your idea to yourself, you can always make the wrinkle private which will then only be able to be viewed by you.

My Brain is a very useful App and I encourage you to try it for yourself. You can view a quick walkthrough below.

This app was made with a Ruby on Rails backend, SQLite3 database, React frontend, HTML, CSS, Bycrpt for user data security, and JWT for User Authentication.
