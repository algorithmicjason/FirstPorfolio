Welcome to the, Food Recipe App

Hundreds of delicious recipes

Say goodbye to hunger because the Food Recipe App is a useful tool for users of all cooking skills. Users can search hundreds of recipes based on the ingredients from their own kitchen, and have food infront of them within minutes!

After opening Food Recipe App simply click the search bar and input the ingredient you wish to find recipes for. In the example above we see the top 10 recipe results from the search term "turkey", along with a picture, estimated prep time, and other ingredients we might need to create the dish.

So say goodbye to hunger and say hello to hundreds of food options using the Food Recipe App! This app was made using HTML, CSS, React, and Edamam search Api.
